Quantity Price Breaks & Limit Purchase App Installation

Directly go to this link: https://apps.shopify.com/quantity-price-breaks-limit-purchase

After you install this app, you need make some modifications in your theme, by through these step:

Step 1: go to shopify admin -> Online store -> Themes -> Edit HTML/CSS

Step 2: Change in theme.liquid file

Goto Layout folder -> theme.liquid

At the end of file you can see body end tag

You need to add {% include "ot-custom-cart" %} on before this tag

Step 3: Change in cart.liquid or cart-template.liquid file

Goto Templates folder -> cart.liquid

Go through this file you can see this line

Note: We need to follow element tag with name="updates[]" attribute

Then add data-id="{{item.id}}" attribute into this tag

About theme, please Goto Sections folder -> cart-template.liquid

Step 4: Disable ajax cart

If you disable ajax cart in your shop then when your customer click to Add To Cart button they will redirect to your cart page immediately. (if your shop already run by this way, so you don't need to do this step )

Disable ajax cart may different in different theme. Here're guide to disable ajax cart in some familiar theme:

Go to shopify Admin -> Online store -> Themes -> Customize theme

Go to Cart page -> Cart type -> Page and Save

App configure Tutorial

After installed Quantity Price Breaks & Limit Purchase App you will be redirect to admin page.

We have 3 main tab:

  • QUANTITY PRICE BREAKS: Manager quantity price breaks feature
  • LIMIT PRODUCT ORDER: Manager limit product order feature
  • STYLE OPTIONS: Manager styles to show

1. QUANTITY PRICE BREAKS

To set quantity price breaks for a product. You need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Click to product you want to set.
Step 2: Select variant of product you want to set.
Step 3: Put quantity break info and save.

In example upper, I just made quantity price breaks of variant: L price is 650.00 VND of product: Product 100 that if customer buy this product variant with quantity 5 or above, the price per product will be 645.00 VND.

In case you want to make quantity price breaks for all variants of a product, you need to do these step again with another variants that you want.

Same on another products.

We provide a table data to show the quantity price breaks automaticity in your product page.

You just put gr0upt4bl3 to your product description or put it in your product template.

2. LIMIT PRODUCT ORDER

To set limit quantity purchase for a product. You need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Click to product you want to set.
Step 2: Select variant of product you want to set.
Step 3: Put limit number and save.

In example upper, I just made the minimum number to purchase of variant: X of product: Product 10 is 2 and maximum is 20.

Then when customer add product variant to cart and the number not valid to our condition, it will appear an error notification and disable checkout button

In case you want to make limit for all variants of a product, you need to do these step again with another variants that you want.

Same on another products.
